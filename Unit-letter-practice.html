Unit 1

Overview:

In this unit, students will be learning how to indtroduce themselves and how to ask about others. Students will learn how to talk about their family members and to give descriptions about them. Students will start to develop their French skills in all four modes of communication; speaking, listening, reading and writing.
We will also be discussing French culture. Some topics we will cover include the French school system and how the French greet each other!
